# ⭐ RGX | Reputation Level-Up! ![RepUp Logo](https://github.com/DonnieDice/ReputationLevelUp/blob/main/media/Textures/icon.tga?raw=true) ## ![](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/images/kiwi.gif?raw=true) RGX Mods - RealmGX Community Project *** ## 🎯 Overview **RGX | Reputation Level-Up!** plays iconic sounds from your favorite games every time you gain Reputation or Renown ranks in World of Warcraft. 40+ game sounds to choose from — all with independent volume controls. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/images/kiwi.gif?raw=true) **The Kiwi Says:** "Honored with the Kiwi! Bwwiiiee." *** ## ✨ Features 🏆 Reputation Ranks - Play sounds from other games every time you gain a Reputation rank! 🌟 Renown Ranks - Play sounds from other games every time you gain a Renown rank! (Retail only) 🎵 40+ Game Sounds - A massive library of sounds from beloved games, including a Random option for variety. 🔊 Independent Volume - Separate volume sliders for Reputation and Renown events. 🎮 Multi-Version - Supports Retail, Cataclysm Classic, and Classic Era. 💾 Persistent Settings - Your sound selections are saved automatically between sessions. **Note:** When new sounds are added they are listed alphabetically. You may need to re-select previously selected sounds after updates. *** ## 🎵 Available Game Sounds
